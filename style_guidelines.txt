Fonts
=====
The "Roboto" font will be used.
The text will be white to contrast with the dark background colors.


Links
=====
There will be two different types of links:
  - LinkedIn profiles, reprensented by a LinkedIn logo in the team members descriptions.
  - Links/buttons in the top bar. Will scroll up/down to the corresponding part of the page.


Animations
==========
Top bar :
  - Merges with the background when fully scrolled up. Becomes black when scrolling down.
  - Stays at the top of the screen even when scrolling down.
  - The buttons will be animated when the user hovers over them.

Team members :
  - When the user hovers over a picture, the description fades in and the image fades out.
