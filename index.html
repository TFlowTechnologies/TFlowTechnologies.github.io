Product

TFlow technologies is a traffic management system meant to help first responders such as paramedics, fire fighters and police officers get to emergencies in a faster and safer way. All emergency vehicles are equipped with a GPS, which shares there location with TFlow. TFlow then makes sure that the lights stay green for the upcoming emergency vehicle. TFlow, gets your first responders first on the scene.

TCertified

Your vehicle is more than just a means of transportation. It is a place that should feel safe and be at the finest edge of technologies. TCertified is a way to discern, a way to know that your car is up to tomorrow's standards. A vehicle that is Tcertified can efficiently alert you when a emergency is happening in your vicinity. It means that the vehicle has been built following firm and precise standards. TCertified is among the only car certification that is recognized by the government.
